  • Account Creation: Users should be able to register for an account by providing their basic details such as name, email address, and password. .
  • Profile Management: Once registered, users should have access to a profile dashboard where they can manage their personal information, including contact details. .

Parking Space Listings:

Provide a comprehensive list of available parking spaces, including information such as location, price, capacity, and availability.

Detailed Information: Each parking space listing should include essential information such as location, address, pricing, capacity (number of vehicles it can accommodate), and any additional amenities or features (e.g., covered parking, valet service, security). The more detailed and accurate the information, the better equipped users will be to make informed decisions.

Login and Authentication:

Implement a secure login system to protect user accounts. This may involve using encryption methods, such as hashing passwords, and incorporating measures like two-factor authentication (2FA) for an extra layer of security.

To book a parking spot online, you can visit our website or use our mobile app. Simply enter your location, date, and time, and browse available parking options. Select the desired spot, proceed to payment, and you will receive a confirmation with the parking details.

Yes, you can change or cancel your parking reservation. Log in to your account on our website or app, go to your bookings, and find the reservation you want to modify.
