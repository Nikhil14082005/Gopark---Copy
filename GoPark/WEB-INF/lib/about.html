Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

To book a parking spot online, you can visit our website or use our mobile app. Simply enter your location, date, and time, and browse available parking options. Select the desired spot, proceed to payment, and you will receive a confirmation with the parking details.

Yes, you can change or cancel your parking reservation. Log in to your account on our website or app, go to your bookings, and find the reservation you want to modify. You can adjust the date, time, or location, or cancel the booking if needed. Please note that there may be specific cancellation policies in place.

We offer various payment methods for your convenience. You can pay online using credit or debit cards, or through mobile payment options like Apple Pay or Google Pay. Select your preferred payment method during the booking process, enter the required details, and complete the transaction.

We strive to ensure accurate availability information, but on rare occasions, there can be discrepancies due to unforeseen circumstances. If you encounter an occupied parking spot, please contact our customer support immediately. We will assist you in finding an alternative spot or provide a refund if necessary.

We prioritize the safety and security of your vehicle. Our partnered parking facilities are equipped with surveillance cameras, proper lighting, and security personnel to enhance safety. However, it's important to note that parking is at your own risk, and we recommend removing any valuable items from your vehicle or securing them out of sight.
